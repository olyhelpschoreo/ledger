# Ledger of Days

*An ops dashboard for a human.* A private-first personal analytics + journaling app.

Track your **mood, sleep, energy, workouts, spending, habits**, and a one-line journal each day — then read a plain-English **weekly/monthly review written from your own data** (trends, your best and hardest day, and correlations like "the mornings after your better nights of sleep, your mood runs higher"), plus a mood-colored year heatmap.

- **Private-first** — everything lives in your browser's local storage. Nothing is ever uploaded.
- **Works offline** — installable PWA; open it and it works with no connection.
- **No account, no tracking, no cost.**

## Run locally

Just open `index.html`, or serve the folder:

```bash
node server.js   # http://localhost:8770
```

Single-file app — all the logic is in `index.html`.
